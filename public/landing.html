FAQ
Questions, answered
Is it really free?
Yes — while the app is in active development, all features are free. No credit card, no trial timer. If we introduce paid plans later, we'll say so clearly and give existing accounts fair notice.
Do I need to install anything?
No. FurniCraft 3D runs entirely in your browser. It works best on a desktop or laptop with a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox or Safari).
Can I use my designs commercially?
Yes. Designs you create are yours. Export the cut sheets and build them for yourself or for clients.
How accurate are the cut sheets?
Dimensions and parts come directly from your 3D model in millimetres. Always double-check critical measurements against your own stock and blade kerf before cutting.
What does the AI assistant actually do?
It proposes a starting design from your description and suggests joinery and materials. You stay in control — every part is fully editable by hand afterwards.
Is my work saved if I close the tab?
Projects you save to your account are stored in the cloud with version history. Unsaved work in a guest session isn't kept, so create a free account to save.
Can I sign in without a password?
Yes. You can sign in with a one-time code sent to your email, or use Google. A traditional email and password also works.
